This is my Axial SCX6 Jeep working through the rougher line behind the shed. The current setup feels planted, but I still want more bite off-camber and a little less chassis slap on the drop-offs.

Notes for the next pass: soften the rear a touch, check the shock preload, and see whether the front tires are folding over too early on the sharper rock edges.
