The bright yellow body is the "before" picture.  I read about salt-chipping and decided to try a rusty paint job on this Isuzu Carry-all truck.



I started with a a few different rust color layers. Then sprayed on a layer of matte clear coat. Then I sprayed some mist on parts I wanted to look chipped then dropped salt on the wet areas and let it dry.



I then airbrushed on the white top coat and let it dry.  Once dry I washed the salt off and the rust was left behind. I gave it some more light blasts with some dirt and dust shades and topped it off with one more coat of matte clear.



Now I want to do another one!
